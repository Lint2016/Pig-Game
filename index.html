How to Play:

  1. Players take turns to roll a die
  2. Each roll gets added to your current score
  3. If you roll a 1, you lose your current points and your turn ends
  4. Use the Hold button to save your points and end your turn
  5. First player to reach 40 points wins!

Tip: Be careful not to get too greedy or you might lose your points!
